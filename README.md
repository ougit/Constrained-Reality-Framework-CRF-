## 🌌 Abstract
The **Constrained Reality Framework (CRF)** proposes that reality is not a fixed substrate but the continuous outcome of a fundamental process: the probabilistic resolution of latent informational possibility under constraint. 

It derives the entirety of existence from a single primitive: **$\delta$ (distinction)**. 

## 🗝️ Key Features & Emergence
From the ground of **$\delta$**, the framework traces the emergence hierarchy of:
* **Time and Constraint:** How the flow of reality is shaped.
* **Mind and Awareness:** The internal resolution of information.
* **Suffering:** Integrated into the framework's fundamental dynamics.
* **Physical Predictions:** * Existence of **no-singularity black holes**.
    * A **testable quantum measurement prediction** that challenges current paradigms.
